AIPS++ E-mail Reflectors

Several electronic mail reflectors are active for AIPS++. To join any of them visit the appropriate mailman-listinfo page at http://listmgr.cv.nrao.edu/mailman/listinfo/aips2-"mailinglist".

The following lists are currently active:

  • aips2: General announcements and reports about AIPS++. This list presently has about one message a month.
  • The following reflectors are likely to be of interest only those actively working on AIPS++:
    • aips2-glish: Discussion relating to Glish.
    • aips2-inhale: The most recent logs of rebuilds of AIPS++ at the various sites.
    • aips2-reports: AIPS++ progress reports, see also aips2-weekly-reports.
    • aips2-sitemgr: Announcements and discussions intended for managers of AIPS++ installations.
    • aips2-developers: A general-purpose reflector for people working on the AIPS++ project.
  • The following reflectors are likely to be of interest only to people at particular sites:
    • aips2-naug: National Radio Astronomy Observatory.
    • aips2-aoc: Array Operations Center (Socorro, NM).
The current set of lists has been trimmed considerably, we do have available archives of old-now-defunct lists as well as messages submitted before Dec 2003.
